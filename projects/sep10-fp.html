Sep10 Freedom Project

The “Freedom Project” for SEP10 is all about choosing a job topic that you’re really passionate about and creating a website on it. The purpose of this blog is to show you the process of how I created a website.

For my project, I am making a website about transportation using HTML, CSS, and Github. This blog will walk through my process of independently studying on Aframe and making the website.

Aframe is a web framework for building virtual reality (VR) experiences and a challenge I had with my project was getting the aframe positioning correctly. A takeaway I got to surpass this challenge is to guess and check until you get the primative to exactly where you want with decimals. My next steps would be trying to recreate software webpages in aframe instead of linking them to a it's website.
